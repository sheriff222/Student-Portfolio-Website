About Me

Hi, my name is **Hassan Sheriff**, and I am presently studying hard to attain a **Diploma in Computer Science**. This study is being undertaken at the renowned **University of Management and Technology (UNIMTECH)**, which is located in the cosmopolitan city of Freetown, Sierra Leone.

Technology has never been to me merely a subject of academic pursuit; it is a canvas upon which I can showcase my creativity, work on significant real-world problems, and positively contribute to society in a meaningful way. I feel that excellent developers are not merely individuals who code; they are the characters of thoughtful creators, ingenious designers, and inspiring leaders.

**Scholarship and Development** I'm in **Year 2** of my diploma, with good academic records, and being actively involved in projects and organizations that push me to develop further. I'm a backend and full-stack developer with increasing expertise in Java, Python, and recent web technologies. My studies have established a solid background in algorithms, system design, and data structures.

**The Individual I Am Beyond the Code Lines** Outside coding, I'm a perpetual learner who sustains on creativity and meaningful storytelling. I enjoy watching movies (especially those that spark imagination), reading inspirational and technical books, and mentoring young students who are new to the world of computing. I am a strong believer in **tech with purpose**.
